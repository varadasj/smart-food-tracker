Understanding the Problem

Every year, around 1.3 billion tons of food is wasted while 828 million people go to bed hungry. This waste also contributes to climate change and resource depletion.

Our mission aligns with SDG 2 (Zero Hunger) and SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption) to promote sustainable and efficient food systems for all.

Our Smart Solution

The AI-Based Smart Food Waste Tracker empowers users to:

  • Upload food images to analyze type and quantity
  • Get suggestions to donate or compost food
  • Locate nearby NGOs/food banks
  • Learn sustainable food habits
